I love to code. The problem, the solution, the search for answers, the obstacles, the hunt for buried treasure...I love it all!!! My inquisitive nature love of learning are what drew me into the far away land of coding, but it's the "art of coding" -- the magic you can create with code, that won't let me go. Finding solutions to problems and then being able to implement those solutions into a useful product through code; Nothing can beat that ! The overwhelming feeling of satisfaction that comes from conquering all of the obstacles on a tough project and achieving the end result of code that is clean,concise, beautifully crafted and the solution to someone's problem--- Well,...the feeling that comes from helping others through code is unbelievable!
